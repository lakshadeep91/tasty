Matthew Jaskiewicz Music


On this website, you can explore the musical journey of a choir and orchestra conductor of over 50 years; and access his extensive library of arrangements.

Most of the scores were written to fill the gaps in the Polish choral repertoire for vocal groups of varying skill levels. All vocal and piano scores are available for free.

For pieces requiring orchestra parts, these can be obtained directly from Matthew for a nominal fee.

About Matthew Jaskiewicz_

____ .

Matthew Jaskiewicz is a renowned music director in Europe and in North America. He received his conducting training at the Warsaw Academy of Music in Poland, and after graduating, he soon established his reputation as a outstanding director with the Warsaw University Choir. The choir’s greatest achievement was winning first prize at the British Middlesborough Choral Competition in 1970. In the 1971-72 season, Matthew directed the award- winning men and boys’ choir, Les Rossignolet de Roubaix, in France. After returning to Poland, Matthew became the conductor of the Warsaw Chamber Opera, where he created the Ars Antiqua Chamber Choir. When the choir moved to the Warsaw Music Society, Matthew became the music director of the series of concerts at various churches in Warsaw.

Biografia Macieja Jaśkiewicza_

____ .

Maciej Jaśkiewicz jest absolwentem Państwowej Wyższej Szkoły Muzycznej (później przemianowanej na Akademię Muzyczną) w Warszawie. Muzyczną działalność rozpoczął w roku 1968 obejmując stanowisko kierownika muzycznego Chóru Akademickiego Uniwersytetu Warszawskiego. W latach 1971-72 prowadził francuski chór chłopięco-męski Les Rossignolet de Roubais. Po powrocie do Warszawy przez 6 lat działał w Warszawskiej Operze Kameralnej, prowadząc liczne przedstawienia operowe i koncerty muzyki chóralno-instrumentalnej w założonym w Operze chórze Ars Antiqua.
